About SummerSet

We Treat Every Nantucket Home Like Our Own

Founded on Nantucket, SummerSet Properties delivers hands-on property management tailored to island living. Our team has called the island home for over a decade, building deep connections with local subcontractors, vendors, and the community.

We blend personal communication with smart-home monitoring technology. You get timely human interactions paired with continuous 24/7 oversight, so your property stays protected whether you are on-island or a thousand miles away.

Lyuben and Atanas lead every project with care, precision, and accountability. As master carpenters and licensed contractors, they bring both craft expertise and management discipline to every home they touch.
